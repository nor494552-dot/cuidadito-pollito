Environmental Awareness Exam - 1st Grade

1. Species Extinction (Mammals, Birds, and more)

1.1 Habitat loss:

Destruction of natural homes.

1.2 Climate change:

Alteration of vital temperatures.

1.3 Invasive species:

Animals that displace local ones.

1.4 Genetic pollution:

Unnatural crossbreeding.

1.5 Diseases:

Viruses attacking vulnerable populations.

1.6 Fragmentation:

Ecosystems divided by roads.

1.7 Illegal trade:

Selling animals as pets.

1.8 Food shortage:

Due to human overexploitation.

1.9 Ocean acidification:

Death of corals and marine life.

1.10 Human indifference:

The main cause of species neglect.
2. Deforestation and Excessive Logging

2.1 Intensive agriculture:

Burning jungles for crops.

2.2 Livestock:

Cleared spaces for grazing.

2.3 Illegal logging:

Stealing wood from protected areas.

2.4 Forest fires:

Caused by humans or extreme droughts.

2.5 Mining:

Soil and tree removal for metals.

2.6 Urbanization:

Building cities in forests.

2.7 Paper and pulp:

Excessive consumption of paper.

2.8 Palm oil:

Destruction of tropical rainforests.

2.9 Soil erosion:

Land that can no longer grow life.

2.10 Loss of oxygen:

Fewer lungs for the world.
